        Are you interested in working at one of the nation's largest and fast paced consulting companies? CuckooNet offers an exciting and friendly workplace for skilled individuals.

     CuckooNet is currently interested in hiring the following positions. Please submit resumes to employment@cuckoo.com.


Network Consultant

Network Consultants work on site or remotely on customers' networks to design and implement complex networks. If you are interested in earning a starting salary between $90 and $100k (not including stock options), please see above information to apply.
  • Requirements:
    • CCIE and at least three years working in an Internet environment with Cisco networking products.
    • Knowledge of gated routing daemon.
    • Knowledge of OSPF, BGP4, RIP, and other common protocols.
    • Knowledge of rwhoisd.
    • Knowledge of firewalls.
  • Pluses:
    • Experience with Ascend and Bay Networks products.
    • Experience with Checkpoint Firewall-1.
  

Systems Consultant

Systems Consultants work on site and remotely on customers' servers to design and implement UNIX and NT systems. If you are interested in earning a starting salary between $110 and $120k (not including stock options), please see above information to apply.
  • Requirements:
    • Seven years UNIX experience.
    • MSIE and three years Win32 experience.
    • Simple programming skills.
    • Knowledge of Solaris, SunOS, IRIX, SCO, BSDI, FreeBSD, Linux, OpenBSD, NetBSD, and XENIX.
  • Pluses:
    • Expert programming skills.
    • Over 10 years UNIX experience.
    • Over 5 years Win32 experience.
  

Programmer

Programmers do contract programming and consulting to design and implement their software at customer's sites. If you are interested in earning a starting salary between $170 and $190k (not including stock options), please see above information to apply.
  • Requirements:
    • Five years programming experience.
    • Knowledge of UNIX and Win32.
    • Knowledge of C, C++, VC++, Perl, UNIX shell scripting, TCL, TK.
  • Pluses:
    • Over ten years programming experience.
    • Assembler on various architectures.
